About Us

My name is Kenneth and I run Unique Contractors, a construction business in Enugu, EN. We specialize in providing a wide range of services,including new construction,renovations,home improvement projects,and repairs. Our team of experienced professionals are knowledgeable in all aspects of the construction process starting from the initial stage,the planning stage to the final completion of projects. Our team is available to answer any questions and provide guidance that our clients may have.
